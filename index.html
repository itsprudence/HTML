Homemade Chocolate Icecream

This is a simple step by step recipe to make chocolate icecream for chocolate lovers.
Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 0 mins
Chill & Freeze Time: 2 hrs
Total:2 hrs 10 mins
Servings: 10 servings
Yield: 1 litre

Many recipes have egg yolk and cooking the mixture on the stove.This has only 5 ingredients.
Let's do this!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps to make it

Step 1:
Gather the ingredients

Step 2:Whisk together the milk, cocoa powder, and sugar. Whisk until the sugar and cocoa completely dissolve.

Step 3: In a large bowl, beat the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.
Fold the whipped cream along with the vanilla extract into the cocoa powder mixture.

Step 4: Refrigerate the mixture for at least 30 minutes
so it is completely cold. This will help it freeze faster,
improve the texture, and allow the cocoa powder
to become fully hydrated by the milk and cream.

Step 5: Give the ice cream base one more gentle stir and freeze
about 1 1/2 to 2 hours.

Step 6: Store your ice cream in an airtight plastic container in the
back of the freezer. This will help preserve the flavor and
texture of the finished ice cream.
